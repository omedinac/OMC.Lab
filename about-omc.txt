Oscar Medina Contreras is an immunologist working on intestinal inmune responses. 

He received his Bsc in Biology in 2003 from State of Mexico Autonomous University, a MSc in 2005 and has PhD in 2009, both from the Center for Research and Advanced Studies. He did a postdoc at the Emory School f Medicine and the Emory Children's Center from 2009-2013, and was a Research Associate in the Center for Inflammation, Immunity & Infection, at the Institute for Biomedical Research, Georgia State University. 

2014 regresó a México como Catedrático CONACyT en el Instituto Nacional de Enfermedades Respiratorias

Since 2015 he has been a Medical Sciences Researcher at the Mexico Children's Hospital, a National Institute of Health, in the Immunology & Proteomics Laboratory and the Epidemiology, Endocrinology & Nutrition Research Unit. 

He has published more than 45 research papers, and has over 1000 cites. He is Member of several research societies, such as the Mexican Society for Immunology, the Society for Mucosal Immunology, the American Society for Investigative Pathology, the Mexican Society for Biochemistry, the American Association of Immunologists, the Crohn’s & Colitis Foundation of America, the Glycosciences for Health, Virology, and Protein Structure Research Networks.
